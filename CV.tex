\section*{Research Interests}
My current research focuses on two directions:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{RL chain-of-thought reasoning:} reinforcement-learning post-training for large models, especially stable training of \texttt{<think>} to better guide \texttt{<answer>}.
    \item \textbf{Human-centered robot decision making:} aligning human intentions, motivations, and demands with robust embodied decision making and robot actions.
\end{itemize}
